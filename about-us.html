About Code and Dexterix

  • DEXTERIX
  • “Dexterix” is inspired and derived from the adjective Dexterous: mentally adroit and skillful.
  • Someone who is adroit is clever and skillful. An adroit leader will be able to persuade people to go with his ideas. An adroit sculptor can turn a lump of clay into an object of great beauty.
  • Dexterix was founded in the year 2014 with the vision to recognize technical skill and intelligence, and encourage innovation on campus.
  • DexteriX has been a huge success since its launch, we are witnessing a tremendous year-on-year growth in number of participants and level of skill and knowledge.
  • We started with events concentrated only on two departments (CS & E and IS), but as time passed we have come to a stage where events are being organized for every department.

The Amazing Story!

  • CODE
  • Objectives
  • The Tech club of Atria Institute of Technology – Community of Developing Engineers – CODE – was instituted and nurtured by the Computer Science & Engineering department in the year 2013, to serve as a forum for creative minds to interact and coordinate on new developments in Science & Technology.
  • To serve as a platform for community members to explore beyond the boundaries of the regular university curriculum.
  • To enable members to come forward with innovative ideas; and implement projects by coordinating with other members and faculty.
  • To encourage hands-on learning and practical work experience.

  • Background
  • In 2013, CODE was founded by CS & E department students: Deepika K S, Farha Asad, Jisha M Chacko, Ashwani M K, Ankit and Anupam Prakash under the inspiration of Mrs. Pavithra B (Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science & Engineering) and guidance of Professor Basavaraju (previously HOD of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering and Dean of the college).
  • Since then the Community has organized several workshops, seminars and certifications in collaboration with different companies, on a huge range of topics such as Web development, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things and Application development. Along with hosting student seminars, webinars and exhibitions for members to showcase projects.
  • The Core committee consisting of the founding members of CODE also established the first Tech Fest of Atria Institute of Technology in the year 2014.
