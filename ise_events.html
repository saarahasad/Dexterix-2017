Tech Quiz

Are you up-to-date??

Students will have to take the preliminary round and only the best 8 will be selected out of the lot. The quiz will be strictly tech based. Eliminations will happen. Team with the maximum number of marks win.

  • Entry: 2 per team
  • Duration: 3 hours of fun
  • Where: Classroom

Cryptography

Put on you detective hat!!

Got skills to decode an encrypted message using your brains? Then this is the event that you mustn’t miss! Rounds will consist of questions with encoded message which the participants will have to decode. There will be only general encryption.

  • Entry: 2 per team
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Where: classroom

Web Design

Engaging, purposeful and creative !!

Students will have to create a webpage/site based on the statement given to them. The team can use any programming language to develop the website. The team with the most innovative/ creative webpage/site, wins.

  • Entry: 2 per team.
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Where: classroom/lab

Start-up Pitch

Start your entrepreneurial journey!!

Got an idea of starting a company? Then this is the event for you! The student will have a platform to talk about their start up idea in front of the panel. The student will have to explain about their company, how to monetize it and how will he/she expand his/her business. He/she will have to have a paper ready on the plan.  

  • Entry: solo event
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Where: Classroom

Meme Generator

Are you the next TROLL KING !!!!

Do you think you have it in you to be the meme king/queen of Atria? Find out by participating in meme generator where you will be competing against the funniest lot. Elimination will be there per round. The best meme will be the winner. P.S. Vulgar memes will be disqualified instantly.

  • Requirement: Student must bring their own device with the “meme generator” app.
  • Entry: solo event
  • Duration: 2 hour
  • Where: Classroom
