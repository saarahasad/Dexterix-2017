Circutrix

Are you a Debugger??

A platform to express the talent in circuit debugging. Occurrence of errors is common in circuits and it is a complicated task debugging them. Here we provide you an opportunity to show off your skills to rectify the glitch in the circuit and make it right.

  • Entry: 2 per team
  • Duration: 2 hours of fun
  • Where:Classroom

Brainiax

Put your Brain to the test

A mind sport to test your knowledge in the vast field of electronics and communication. Mind bogging questions from basic concepts of electronics to high end technologies of the industry.

  • Entry: 2 per team
  • Duration: 2 hours of fun
  • Where: Classroom

Ram Rom Evoke

if you know it .. u win it !!

Know everything about electronics!?? Gear up!  Put all your wires on the right place. Test yourself. Guess it right by identifying each thing shown. Make sure you don’t run off time!

  • Entry: 2 per team.
  • Duration: 3 hours of fun
  • Where: classroom

Advertise it

Can you convince us??

Have great enacting skills and technical knowledge??  Reach the judges mind body and soul by advertising the electronic component by adding up all the pros of the given electronic or electrical concept.  

  • Props limit: 2 per team (should be got by the team)
  • Entry: 2 per team
  • Duration: 32 hours of fun
  • Where: Classroom

Typo Interface

Debug and Interface

They would have to clear all errors in the time limit. Top teams would qualify to the next round. It could be C or C++, microprocessor or microcontroller. They would be given programs with errors which should be debugged and then interfaced. Fastest team doing it would win.

  • Entry: solo event
  • Duration: 3 hour of fun
  • Where: Classroom

DOTA 2 - Tournament

The Game is on !!

Competition method: 5 versus 5 [Team play]
Game mode: Captain's Mode (CM)
Sides: (Radiant / Dire) will be determined by the host.
Victory condition: The first team to destroy the other team’s ancient.
LOBBY SETTINGS: Mode: Captains Mode (All Pick when a rehost is needed [same hero, same player])
Server: Singapore
Password: will be given during the tournament.
SPECIFIC RULES: During the tournament all members team must have their team tag during the event. Players must be prepared before their scheduled games. All competitors must be present 15 minutes prior to their scheduled match. Teams who fail to join the room at the first fifteen (15) minutes from the start of their schedules will automatically lose at the game one (1) of their match series. (excuses are not entertained, even if the game is updating, etc.) Teams who fail to join the room at the next fifteen (15) minutes will automatically lose the whole series (game of three matches). Players who are registered must be the one to play. If a reserve is needed, only the listed reserves must also play. Final calls are irrevocable.

  • Entry: 5-7 players per team (5 main, 2 reserves)]
  • Duration: Two full Days!!
  • Where: Classroom
